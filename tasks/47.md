#### код с коментариями:
```sql
SELECT 
    CITY.NAME
FROM CITY INNER JOIN COUNTRY
    ON CITY.COUNTRYCODE = COUNTRY.CODE
    WHERE COUNTRY.CONTINENT = 'Africa';
```
